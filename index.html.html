What is Capital Financeability?
Capital Financeability measures how readily a project can attract and close debt & equity from institutional investors — banks, infrastructure funds, and development finance institutions (CEFC, ARENA, NAIF).

It reflects bankable offtake agreements, technology readiness (TRL), regulatory clarity, and lender familiarity with the asset class.

Very High = financing closes today. Low = government guarantee required.
